Chattanooga's Sloppy Street Tacos

Sloppy Street Tacos is an indie/alternative rock band known for their eclectic sound and high-energy performances. The group features Ben Daugherty on guitars, synths, and tape loops; Brett Tallent on bass; Neal Brite on guitar and saxophone; and Hall Hastings on drums.

About

Hailing from Chattanooga, Tennessee, they have played at local venues such as JJ’s Bohemia, Barrelhouse Ballroom, and HiFi Clydes on Main, building a reputation for their dynamic and experimental approach to rock music. Their music blends elements of funk, rock, and psychedelic influences, making them a standout act in the regional music scene.
